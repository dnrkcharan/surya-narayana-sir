About Dr. Reddy

Higher education leadership, built around measurable growth.

Dr. M.R.S. Suryanarayana Reddy is a higher education leader, academic strategist, and institutional growth expert with more than 18 years of experience across admissions strategy, institutional branding, academic governance, student recruitment, and organizational development. He currently serves as Professor & Director - Admissions & Brand Building at Vishwa Vishwani Institute of Systems & Management, Hyderabad.

His work connects admissions transformation, digital marketing, CRM-led decision-making, stakeholder engagement, curriculum development, quality assurance, rankings, outreach, and industry collaboration. Across progressive leadership roles, he has built feeder-institution networks, strengthened partnerships, improved visibility, and helped institutions create accountable systems for enrolment and growth.

A Ph.D. in Human Resource Development from JNTUA, Dr. Reddy is also a researcher, speaker, mentor, Board of Studies and Academic Council contributor, Study in India Nodal Officer, and certified Institution Innovation Ambassador. His profile includes 10+ research publications, 16+ conference papers, and recognitions spanning admissions leadership, higher education, marketing strategy, and Human Resources.
